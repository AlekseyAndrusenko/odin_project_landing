This website is about Thor, the Thunderer

Thor, known as the thunderer, is a prominent deity in Norse mythology, wielding his mighty hammer Mjölnir to command thunder and lightning. As the son of Odin, the Allfather, Thor is revered for his strength, courage, and protection of both gods and humans against the forces of chaos. With his red beard and chariot drawn by magical goats, Thor embodies the power of storms and is a symbol of protection, fertility, and the wild forces of nature in Norse folklore.
